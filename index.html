Process

How the Work Gets Made

No formula. Just a rhythm that starts with a feeling and ends on canvas.

01

Feel

Music, anxiety, restlessness, and the everyday feelings of youth supply the first spark.

02

Sketch

Fine liners, pencil, and quick studies work out the shape of a new composition.

03

Layer

Spray paint, acrylic, wire, and posca marker are built up across canvas, Masonite, and paper.

04

Let It Go

The message doesn't have to land the way it was meant to. Art is too personal for that.
